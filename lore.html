The Lore of the C.I.A

The Central Intelligence Archives (C.I.A) was founded after the First Reality Breach. Tasked with maintaining order, containment, and study of anomalous entities, the organization operates across all sectors of known existence.

Core Divisions

  • Containment Division: Responsible for capturing and securing entities.
  • Research Division: Studies anomalies for strategic and technological gain.
  • Field Operations: Executes on-site containment and cleanup missions.
