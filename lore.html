The Origins of the Central Intelligence Archives

The Central Intelligence Archives were founded following the first recorded multiversal breach — an event that merged time, space, and reality itself.

Tasked with documenting and containing entities from alternate realms, the C.I.A operates across timelines to preserve order and secrecy.

Divisions

  • Containment Division: Manages active anomalies and secure facilities.
  • Research Division: Analyzes dimensional rifts, artifacts, and entity biology.
  • Field Division: Conducts reconnaissance and first contact operations.

The Archives’ agents are trained to remain unseen, unheard, and unrecorded — the silent protectors of humanity’s fragile reality.
