C.I.A Lore Overview

The Central Intelligence Archives were established after the first recorded multiversal breach, when entities began crossing between realities. The organization was founded to document, study, and contain these phenomena.

Over time, agents discovered that each universe holds unique laws of physics and magic. The Archives operate across these dimensions to protect humanity and preserve balance.

Divisions within the CIA include:

  • Containment Division: Responsible for neutralizing threats and maintaining secrecy.
  • Research Division: Studies interdimensional phenomena and anomalies.
  • Field Agents: Handle direct investigations and first contact situations.

Each case logged within the Archives represents a significant threat or unexplained occurrence, often beyond human understanding.
