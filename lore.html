Founding

The C.I.A was established following the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, when the U.S. government discovered traces of non-human biological material in contaminated zones. This discovery led to the creation of a covert division tasked with monitoring cryptics, anomalies, and extra-terrestrial entities.

The Immortal Virus

In 2047, a secondary viral strain — codenamed IMMORTALS — began surfacing within isolated populations. The C.I.A initiated Protocol I-57, restricting all public access to the affected regions. Containment efforts remain ongoing.

Present Operations

As of 2057, the agency continues to research dimensional instability, cryptic evolution, and unexplained temporal anomalies. Global exposure remains low, but threat potential is classified as Volatile.
