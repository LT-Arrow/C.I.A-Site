Field Handbook

This handbook outlines protocols, ranking systems, and emergency measures for field agents.

Agent Classification

  • Level 1: Basic Clearance — civilian support and field observation.
  • Level 2: Tactical Clearance — operational support and minor containment.
  • Level 3: High Clearance — direct containment and research roles.

Containment Protocols

When encountering unidentified anomalies, agents are to secure the area, mark all exits, and await confirmation from HQ before engagement.
