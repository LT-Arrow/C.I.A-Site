Field Handbook

Welcome, Agent. This manual provides the foundation for all new recruits joining the Central Intelligence Archives.

Code of Conduct

  • Maintain secrecy at all times — leaks are grounds for immediate termination.
  • Follow all directives from higher clearance officers.
  • Never engage an unknown entity without approval.

Operational Protocols

Agents are expected to document all encounters using official Watchlog formatting. Failure to log properly may result in disciplinary action.
