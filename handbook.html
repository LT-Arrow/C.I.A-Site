Welcome to the Cryptic Intelligence Agency

Congratulations, recruit. You have been officially inducted into one of the most secretive branches of global defense. Your mission is to observe, investigate, and contain phenomena that defy logic or science.

Rules of Engagement

  • Maintain discretion. The public must not be aware of cryptic activity.
  • Never engage an entity without clearance from Command.
  • All field data must be submitted via the Anomaly Report Form.
  • In the event of contamination or exposure, await extraction and follow decontamination protocol 47-B.

Security Clearances

Access levels are granted based on operational record and psychological stability. Unauthorized access to classified files will result in termination of clearance and immediate detainment.
