Cryptic-019: “KRAMPUS”

Classification/status: Uncontained / Seasonal Anomalous Predator

Description: Cryptic-019 is a towering, horned humanoid creature resembling Alpine folklore depictions of “Krampus.” The entity manifests annually beginning December 1st and remains active until December 31st. Krampus targets individuals deemed “morally corrupt,” though its criteria remain unknown. Victims are abducted, whipped, or found suspended from trees by iron chains.Highly aggressive Teleports between snow-covered locations Demonstrates superhuman speed Uses rusted chains and hooks as weapons

Containment Notes: Krampus has evaded every attempt at containment. “Hot Spot Activity Zones” are updated daily each December. Agents should not attempt solo engagement.

Cryptic-042: “THE YETI”"

Classification/status:Contained Sub-Arctic Cryptid

Description: Cryptic-042 resembles a massive bipedal ape covered in white fur. Its body temperature remains at −12°C, yet it shows no signs of hypothermia. The creature emits deep, resonant vocalizations capable of stunning prey.

Containment Unit:Refrigeration Cell 4-B, Temperature: −20°C Feeding Schedule: Bi-weekly (frozen protein blocks)

Behavior: The Yeti is territorial but cooperative with researchers when provided cold enrichment. Attempts to move it to warmer zones resulted in violent reactions, including bending reinforced steel.

Cryptic-066: “GINGERBREAD MEN”

Classification/status: Contained/Animated Confectionery Hiver

Description: Cryptic-066 consists of a colony of 14 living gingerbread humanoids. They communicate via high-pitched giggles and smell strongly of cinnamon. Each instance is capable of rapid movement and minor mischief, usually involving sabotage of kitchen equipment or stealing sugary foods.

Containment unit: Confectionery Containment Chamber (CCC-2) Warning: When exposed to heat above 200°F, they multiply. When exposed to milk, they become lethargic for several hours.

Cryptic-101: “Santa Claus

Classification/status: Safe — Approved Roaming Rights/Benevolent Dimensional Entity

Description:Cryptic-101 appears as a large, elderly man with a white beard wearing red winter clothing. He exhibits reality-bending capabilities including teleportation, object generation, and omnilingual communication.

Behavior:: Extremely friendly. Delivers gifts to staff and expresses disappointment when encountering hostility. Displays knowledge of agent identities without prior introduction. Notes: Has been seen conversing with hostile anomalies who refuse to attack him. Allowed to wander designated facility zones from Dec 20th–Dec 26th. Requests for cookies must not exceed 3 per encounter.

C-017 — "Hollow Mothman"

Classification: Threat-Level Amber

Description: Winged humanoid. Emits a frequency causing hallucinations. Appears during electrical disturbances.

Containment Protocol: Contained in a Faraday-isolated chamber. Any power fluctuation requires emergency stabilization.
