Cryptic-019: “KRAMPUS”

Classification: Uncontained / Seasonal Anomalous Predator

Description: Cryptic-019 is a towering, horned humanoid creature resembling Alpine folklore depictions of “Krampus.” The entity manifests annually beginning December 1st and remains active until December 31st. Krampus targets individuals deemed “morally corrupt,” though its criteria remain unknown. Victims are abducted, whipped, or found suspended from trees by iron chains.Highly aggressive Teleports between snow-covered locations Demonstrates superhuman speed Uses rusted chains and hooks as weapons

Containment Notes: Krampus has evaded every attempt at containment. “Hot Spot Activity Zones” are updated daily each December. Agents should not attempt solo engagement.

C-017 — "Hollow Mothman"

Classification: Threat-Level Amber

Description: Winged humanoid. Emits a frequency causing hallucinations. Appears during electrical disturbances.

Containment Protocol: Contained in a Faraday-isolated chamber. Any power fluctuation requires emergency stabilization.
