Operational Directives

Below are current CIA operational directives for all active agents across multiversal sectors. These orders are confidential and to be followed without deviation unless authorized by a senior officer.

Directive 001: Dimensional Stability

Ensure all portals and rifts are stabilized before field departure. Report any distortion beyond level 3 immediately.

Directive 002: Anomaly Classification

All unidentified entities must be tagged and recorded in the Watchlogs before further study or containment.

Directive 003: Agent Conduct

Maintain secrecy. Breaches of protocol will result in immediate suspension and memory audit.
