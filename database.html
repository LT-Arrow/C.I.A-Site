Codename: The Director

Name: Arrow Hawkins

Rank/clearance: director/Alpha Clearance

Division: Central Command Division

Status:alive

Background:A stoic and calculating leader with a knack for seeing three moves ahead. Hawkins maintains an intimidating composure—rarely showing emotion and known to question every motive, even from allies. Beneath the disciplined exterior lies a man haunted by the cost of secrecy and betrayal. He values loyalty but distrusts blind obedience, often encouraging agents to think like their enemies. His leadership style is direct, tactical, and quietly manipulative when needed.
